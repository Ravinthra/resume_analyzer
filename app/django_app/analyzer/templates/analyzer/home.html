How It Works

Three Steps to Better Applications

Our pipeline uses transformer-based NLP to deeply understand both your resume and the target job description.

1

Upload Resume

Drop your resume (PDF or TXT). Our text extraction pipeline handles complex layouts, tables, and multi-column formats using pdfplumber.

2

BERT Analysis

Your resume is tokenized and passed through a fine-tuned BERT model. We compute [CLS] embeddings for semantic understanding and cosine similarity against the job description.

3

Get Insights

Receive your match score, predicted role, matching & missing skills, resume strengths, and actionable improvement suggestions — all in seconds.
